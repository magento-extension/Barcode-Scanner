# Magento 2 Barcode Scanner Extension
Optimize your store with this Magento 2 Barcode Scanner extension

The Magento 2 barcode scanner extension offers a lot of features which will help optimizing your store. Using the Check Shipments option, products can only be shipped if they have been scanned, which minimizes mistakes and will increase the satisfaction rate of your customers. Furthermore, products are easily linked to Magento with help of the Scan Products option, and orders are effortlessly identified with barcodes too using the Scan Orders option. This will make your shipment process much more efficient, and ease your  support office and stock management, while you always get a perfect overview. Those features are easily implemented, since importing and exporting CSV files is done smoothly. In addition, the barcode scanner extension offers a lot of nice features which will help you customize it to meet all of your preferences. In this way, you are enabled to save time, improve efficiency and perfectly control your shipment process.
